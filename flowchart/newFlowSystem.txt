| Step                                      | Actor     | Action                                                       | System Behavior                                                                                           | Data Involved                                          |
| ----------------------------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **1️⃣ Patient books appointment**         | Patient   | Fills appointment form (selects doctor, date, reason).       | Inserts a new record in `appointments` table with `status = 'Pending'`.                                   | `appointments`, `patients`, `doctors`                  |
| **2️⃣ Doctor approves appointment**       | Doctor    | Views pending appointments and clicks “Approve” or “Reject.” | Updates `appointments.status` → `'Approved'` or `'Rejected'`.                                             | `appointments`                                         |
| **3️⃣ Patient sees approval**             | Patient   | Logs in to dashboard → sees list of their appointments.      | Dashboard filters: `WHERE patient_id = ? AND status = 'Approved'`.                                        | `appointments`, `users`                                |
| **4️⃣ Doctor creates paper prescription** | Doctor    | Writes prescription physically (for now).                    | None (manual step).                                                                                       | —                                                      |
| **5️⃣ Assigned secretary digitizes it**   | Secretary | Logs in → clicks "Add Prescription" for that appointment.    | Inserts record into `prescriptions` table (`appointment_id`, `patient_id`, `doctor_id`, `notes`, `date`). | `prescriptions`, `appointments`, `patients`, `doctors` |
| **6️⃣ Patient sees digital prescription** | Patient   | Views prescription list in dashboard.                        | System joins `prescriptions` + `appointments` to show all their prescriptions.                            | `prescriptions`, `appointments`, `users`               |
